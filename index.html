Lisa and Dan

How they met

One sweltering August night, two young Jews swiped right. They met for dinner, And thought, “this one might be the winner.” Okay, no more time for cheesy rhymes. The conversation was easy and they made each other laugh. Dinner dates turned into basketball, kayaking, hiking in Shenanadoah, and most recently, an Italian adventure that led to why you’re all on this website today.
