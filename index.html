Our Story

One sweltering August night,
Two young Jews swiped right.
They met for drinks and dinner,
And thought, this one might be the winner.
The conversation was easy and we made each other laugh. Dinner dates turned into basketball, kayaking, hiking in Shenandoah, and most recently, an Italian adventure that led to why you're on this website today. We are delighted to be sharing our wedding weekend with you and look forward to writing the rest of the story together!
