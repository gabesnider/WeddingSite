Venue the National Arboretum

The National Arboretum is an oasis in our nation's capital. Located in Northeast DC and bordered by the Anacostia River to its east, it is one of the largest arboretums in the county. Congress established the grounds in 1927, and after a series of negotiations, 22 34-foot tall sandstone Corinthian columns, vestiges of the Capitol building's expansion in the 50s, were moved from storage and set atop a knoll, where they remain today. Even though it is open to the public, many people — including DC natives — are unaware of the majestic arboretum. We are thrilled to be celebrating our wedding with you in this special spot.

For more information, please visit The National Park Service website
